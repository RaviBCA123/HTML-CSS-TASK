One of my favorite inventors is Nikola Tesla. He was a brilliant engineer and inventor best known for his contributions to the development of alternating current (AC) electricity. Born in 1856 in what is now Croatia, Tesla's innovations laid the foundation for modern electrical power systems. He also worked on radio, wireless transmission, and even early ideas for radar and robotics. Though he struggled financially, his visionary ideas changed the world and inspired generations of scientists and inventors.
